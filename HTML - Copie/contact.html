frequently asked questions

What is WatchMe ?

WatchMe is a video-streaming platform that offers a fun, social way to watch people play games. Through the WatchMe app (and online at WatchMe.tv), gamers who broadcast their matches (known as streamers) play their favorite titles while providing running commentary on the action.

Is it free to create a WatchMe account?

Does it Cost Money to Use WatchMe? No, it does not cost anything to watch WatchMe or to stream on WatchMe. Creating an account and watching your favorite streamer is totally free and the only time you will have to pay anything is if you choose to subscribe to a channel or donate to a streamer.

What is so special about WatchMe?

WatchMe is streamed live

One of the main things that sets WatchMe apart from other platforms is that it is streamed live. Streaming live is so cool because it makes the experience between the streamer and the viewer much more authentic.
